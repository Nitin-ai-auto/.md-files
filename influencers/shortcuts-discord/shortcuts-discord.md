Why Use Keyboard Shortcuts in Discord?
--------------------------------------

Using keyboard shortcuts in Discord can vastly improve your productivity and streamline your communication efforts. Instead of fumbling through menus and clicking buttons, you can perform tasks faster with a simple keystroke. Whether you're managing a server, chatting with friends, or streaming gameplay, mastering these shortcuts can enhance your overall experience on Discord.

Essential Discord Shortcuts
---------------------------

### General Shortcuts

*    Toggle Mute: **Ctrl + Shift + M** (Windows) / **Command + Shift + M** (Mac) - Quickly mute or unmute your microphone during a voice chat. 
*    Toggle Deafen: **Ctrl + Shift + D** (Windows) / **Command + Shift + D** (Mac) - Instantly deafen yourself to avoid distractions without leaving the chat. 
*    Open User Settings: **Ctrl + ,** (Windows) / **Command + ,** (Mac) - Access your user settings to customize your Discord experience. 

### Chat Shortcuts

*    Quick Switch Channels: **Ctrl + K** - Jump between channels quickly; a time-saver during a bustling conversation. 
*    Mark as Read: **Esc** - Dismiss notifications and mark messages as read with ease. 
*    Search Messages: **Ctrl + F** - Utilize the search function to find specific messages or users in a channel. 

### Voice Channel Shortcuts

*    Push to Talk: You can set a keybind for Push to Talk in User Settings. - Provides a direct way to communicate without affecting your mic settings. 
*    Increase or Decrease Volume: **Ctrl + Up/Down Arrow** - Adjust the voice channel volume without needing to access the settings menu. 

### Advanced Shortcuts

*    Create New Server: **Ctrl + Shift + N** - Quickly set up a new Discord server right from your keyboard. 
*    Switch between Servers: **Alt + 1-9** - Navigate through your servers with ease; a must for those participating in multiple communities. 

Adding Custom Shortcuts
-----------------------

Discord allows users to customize their shortcuts. This flexibility enables you to create a configuration that best suits your workflow. To add your shortcuts:

1.   Navigate to User Settings and select Keybinds.
2.   Click on Add a Keybind and choose the action you want to bind.
3.   Press your desired key combination, and click on Stop Recording to finalize.
