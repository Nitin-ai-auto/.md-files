Essential TikTok Shortcuts You Need to Know
-------------------------------------------

### 1. Speeding Up Video Creation

To adjust the speed of your video, tap the "Speed" icon before recording. You can choose 0.3x, 0.5x, 1x, or even up to 3x for a fast-paced effect.

### 2. Quick Access to Tools

Swipe left or right after capturing a video to quickly access different filters. This shortcut allows you to experiment with various effects without needing to leave the record screen.

### 3. Copy and Paste Clips

Press and hold a clip in your timeline to bring up the option to copy it. This is particularly useful for repeating scenes or effects throughout your video.

### 4. Adding Text Efficiently

On the editing screen, tap the text button, type your text, and use the shortcut “Ctrl + Z” on a keyboard to undo mistakes effortlessly while editing.

### 5. Resizing and Positioning Media

To resize your videos, place two fingers on the video and pinch in or out. This allows for quick adjustments without accessing deep menu settings.

Advanced TikTok Shortcuts for the Tech-Savvy
--------------------------------------------

### 1. Keyboard Shortcuts

If you are editing on a desktop using TikTok's web version, several keyboard shortcuts can save you time. For instance, use “Ctrl + S” to save drafts quickly.

### 2. Editing Audio with Precision

Tap the audio icon to access the audio settings quickly. Use the shortcuts to adjust volume levels, fade in/out transitions, and trim audio clips without affecting the video.
