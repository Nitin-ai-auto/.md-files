3. Essential Jasper Shortcuts
-----------------------------

### 3.1 General Shortcuts

*   Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + P: Access the command panel to quickly switch between tasks or commands.
*   Cmd/Ctrl + K: Insert links into your text seamlessly.

### 3.2 Content Formatting Shortcuts

*   Cmd/Ctrl + B: Bold selected text.
*   Cmd/Ctrl + I: Italicize selected text.
*   Cmd/Ctrl + U: Underline selected text.

These basic formatting shortcuts make content customization more accessible, allowing you to maintain a dynamic and engaging document.

### 3.3 Commands for Enhanced Creativity

*   /blog: Generate a comprehensive blog post outline.
*   /ad: Create persuasive ad copy tailored to your target audience.
*   /story: Start a narrative that Jasper will continue based on your initial guidelines.

These creative commands enable you to leverage Jasper's AI effectively, transforming your ideas into structured content effortlessly.
