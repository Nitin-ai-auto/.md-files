Essential Adobe XD Shortcuts
----------------------------

### Selection and Navigation

*   Select All: `Cmd + A` (Mac) / `Ctrl + A` (Windows)
*   Deselect All: `Shift + Cmd + A` (Mac) / `Shift + Ctrl + A` (Windows)
*   Zoom In/Out: `Cmd + +` / `Cmd + -` (Mac) / `Ctrl + +` / `Ctrl + -` (Windows)
*   Pan (Hand Tool): `Spacebar`

Navigating your design seamlessly is crucial. Use the shortcuts above to move quickly between different sections of your project without losing focus.

### Text Formatting

*   Bold: `Cmd + B` (Mac) / `Ctrl + B` (Windows)
*   Italic: `Cmd + I` (Mac) / `Ctrl + I` (Windows)
*   Underline: `Cmd + U` (Mac) / `Ctrl + U` (Windows)

Quickly formatting your text is essential for maintaining visual clarity and consistency in your designs.

### Layers and Objects

*   Group: `Cmd + G` (Mac) / `Ctrl + G` (Windows)
*   Ungroup: `Shift + Cmd + G` (Mac) / `Shift + Ctrl + G` (Windows)
*   Bring Forward: `Cmd + ]` (Mac) / `Ctrl + ]` (Windows)
*   Send Backward: `Cmd + [` (Mac) / `Ctrl + [` (Windows)

Managing layers effectively can make a significant difference in complex design projects. These shortcuts help you arrange components efficiently.

### Prototyping

*   Play Prototype: `Cmd + Enter` (Mac) / `Ctrl + Enter` (Windows)
*   Toggle Prototype Mode: `Cmd + Tab` (Mac) / `Ctrl + Tab` (Windows)

Test and present your prototypes easily using these shortcuts to switch between design and prototype mode.

Customizing Shortcuts in Adobe XD
---------------------------------

Although Adobe XD comes with predefined shortcuts, you have the flexibility to customize them according to your preferences. You can do this by going to `Preferences > Shortcuts`, allowing you to tailor your workflow for maximum efficiency.
