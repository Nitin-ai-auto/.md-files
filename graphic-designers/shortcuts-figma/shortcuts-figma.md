Why Use Shortcuts in Figma?
---------------------------

Utilizing shortcuts in Figma can significantly enhance your productivity. Here are a few reasons why you should consider adopting shortcuts in your design process:

*   **Speed:** Quickly access tools and functions without navigating through menus.
*   **Efficiency:** Reduce your reliance on the mouse, which can slow you down.
*   **Focus:** Stay immersed in your design without interruptions caused by switching between tools.

Essential Figma Shortcuts for Beginners
---------------------------------------

### 1. Basic Navigation Shortcuts

*   Zoom In: `Ctrl + +` (Cmd + + on Mac)
*   Zoom Out: `Ctrl + -` (Cmd + - on Mac)
*   Fit to Screen: `Shift + 1`
*   View All: `Shift + 2`

Navigating your design canvas becomes seamless with these shortcuts, allowing you to zoom in and out swiftly.

### 2. Working with Layers

*   Group Layers: `Ctrl + G` (Cmd + G on Mac)
*   Ungroup Layers: `Ctrl + Shift + G` (Cmd + Shift + G on Mac)
*   Bring to Front: `Ctrl + Shift + ]` (Cmd + Shift + ] on Mac)
*   Send to Back: `Ctrl + Shift + [` (Cmd + Shift + [ on Mac)

These shortcuts empower you to arrange and organize your layers easily, speeding up your workflow.

### 3. Text and Typography Shortcuts

*   Bold Text: `Ctrl + B` (Cmd + B on Mac)
*   Italic Text: `Ctrl + I` (Cmd + I on Mac)
*   Align Left: `Ctrl + Shift + L` (Cmd + Shift + L on Mac)
*   Align Center: `Ctrl + Shift + C` (Cmd + Shift + C on Mac)

Mastering these text shortcuts will help you manipulate typography quickly.

Intermediate Figma Shortcuts for Enhanced Workflow
--------------------------------------------------

### 1. Working with Components

*   Create Component: `Ctrl + Alt + K` (Cmd + Option + K on Mac)
*   Detach Instance: `Ctrl + Shift + B` (Cmd + Shift + B on Mac)

Using components effectively can streamline your design process, and these shortcuts will help you manage them swiftly.

### 2. Alignment and Distribution Shortcuts

*   Align Horizontal Centers: `Alt + H` (Option + H on Mac)
*   Distribute Horizontally: `Alt + D` (Option + D on Mac)

These shortcuts can help create visually appealing designs by ensuring everything is aligned correctly.

Advanced Figma Shortcuts for Power Users
----------------------------------------

### 1. Prototyping Shortcuts

*   Open Prototype Preview: `Ctrl + P` (Cmd + P on Mac)
*   Toggle Prototype Mode: `Ctrl + Tab`

Being proficient in prototyping shortcuts can improve your workflow when creating interactive designs.

### 2. Collaboration Shortcuts

*   Comment Mode: `C`
*   Share File: `Ctrl + Alt + S` (Cmd + Option + S on Mac)

These shortcuts facilitate quicker collaboration, allowing teams to give and receive feedback efficiently.
