FAQs

**1. What are the eligibility requirements for the Deliveroo Affiliate Program?**

To be eligible, you must have at least 20 followers on any social media platform and create content relevant to food or lifestyle.

**2. How and when will I receive my payments?**

Payments are processed through PayPal on a monthly basis, provided you meet the minimum payout threshold.

**3. What is the approval process like?**

The approval process usually takes 3–5 days. Make sure your social media profiles are active and meet the criteria for better chances of approval.

**4. Are there any specific content requirements?**

You should create authentic and engaging content that promotes Deliveroo's services, including using your unique affiliate link.

**5. What support is available for affiliates?**

The program offers dedicated support, access to a community of influencers, and marketing materials to help you in your promotions.
