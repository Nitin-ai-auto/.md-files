Introduction

SSENSE is an upscale fashion retailer renowned for its mix of luxury, streetwear, and avant-garde brands. Recognizing the significant role influencers play in connecting brands to consumers, the SSENSE Affiliate Program aims at empowering influencers to earn while promoting stylish and high-quality products.

------------------

1.   **Sign Up:** Initiate your application through the SSENSE affiliate link provided.
2.   **Build a Strategy:** Choose the best platforms to share your links (Instagram and TikTok are highly recommended).
3.   **Create Content:** Develop engaging content featuring SSENSE products and share it with your audience.
4.   **Analyze Performance:** Utilize the dashboard to track which products perform best and adjust your strategy accordingly.

FAQs
----

**1. What is the eligibility criteria for joining the SSENSE Affiliate Program?**

To join, you need a minimum of 20 followers on any social media platform and an active social presence.

**2. How and when will I receive payments?**

Payments are made monthly via PayPal once you reach the minimum payout threshold of $50.

**3. What is the approval process duration?**

The approval process typically takes 3–5 days following the submission of your application.

**4. Are there specific content requirements I need to follow?**

While there are no strict requirements, high-quality visuals and engaging storytelling that align with SSENSE's brand image are encouraged.

SSENSE provides a comprehensive dashboard, marketing materials, and dedicated affiliate support for solving queries or concerns.
