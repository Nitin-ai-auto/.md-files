**1. What are the eligibility requirements?**

You need to have a minimum of 10 followers on social media and an active presence on platforms like Instagram, YouTube, or TikTok.

**2. When will I receive my payment?**

Payments are typically processed monthly through PayPal or bank transfer, depending on the affiliate's preference.

**3. How long does the approval process take?**

The application review takes between 3 to 5 days. You'll receive an email confirmation once your application is approved.

**4. Are there specific content requirements for promotions?**

While there are no strict content requirements, it is encouraged to create engaging and authentic content that aligns with your personal brand and the products from The Bay.

**5. What support is available for affiliates?**

Affiliates have access to a dedicated support team and an affiliate dashboard that provides performance insights and promotional materials.
