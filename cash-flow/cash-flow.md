Cash Flow FAQs
--------------

### What is cash flow?



Cash flow is the movement of money in and out of your business. It includes all earnings, expenses, and investments. Keeping track of cash flow helps you stay in control of your finances.

### Why should creators focus on cash flow?



Cash flow gives you a clear picture of your financial health, helping you plan for expenses, reinvest in your work, and avoid financial stress.

### How does a cash flow statement work?



A cash flow statement breaks down all cash inflows and outflows, showing how money moves through your business. It’s a snapshot of your financial activity over a specific period.

### What is free cash flow?



Free cash flow is the money left after covering all expenses and investments. It’s a great way to measure how much you can reinvest or save.
