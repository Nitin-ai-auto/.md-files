Essential Procreate Shortcuts for Beginners
-------------------------------------------

### 1. Navigation Shortcuts

*   **Zoom In/Out:** Pinch to zoom or use the two-finger tap to quickly zoom in and out for precise detailing.
*   **Undo/Redo:** Tap with two fingers to undo, while three fingers will redo your last action. This is crucial for trying out new ideas without the fear of making permanent changes.

### 2. Brush and Color Shortcuts

*   **Select and Change Brush:** Double-tap on the brush icon to quickly open the brush library.
*   **Quickly Change Color:** Use the Color Palette with one tap on the circular color wheel, allowing for rapid color changes while maintaining your creative flow.

### 3. Layer Management Shortcuts

*   **Add a New Layer:** Use the layer menu and tap the plus icon or pinch apart layers with a two-finger gesture.
*   **Merge Layers:** Slide left on a layer and tap on the Merge option for quick adjustments and simplifications.

Advanced Shortcuts for Intermediate Users
-----------------------------------------

### 1. Gesture Controls

*   **QuickMenu:** Use a four-finger tap to bring up the QuickMenu for rapid access to frequently used actions.
*   **Streamline Selection:** Use the Freehand Selection Tool with a four-finger tap and stretch and draw around the area you wish to select effortlessly.

### 2. Custom Shortcut Setup

*   **Custom Actions:** Go to Actions > Prefs > Gesture Controls. Here, you can assign custom actions to specific gestures, allowing for a tailored experience.

Time-Saving Tips for Expert Users
---------------------------------

To maximize productivity, expert users should consider integrating external tools, like keyboard shortcuts and stylus buttons. Pair Procreate with keyboard shortcuts for your computer for an integrated workflow, or assign specific actions to your stylus buttons to minimize screen interruptions.
