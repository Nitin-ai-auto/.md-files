Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

### 1. How do I apply for the Express Influencer Program?

Visit the official Express ambassador site to complete the application form.

### 2. What are the requirements to join?

Requirements typically include a social media presence and engagement metrics that align with brand standards.

### 3. How are influencers compensated?

Influencers can earn a percentage back through Express Cash based on their tier level and sales generated.

### 4. Can I join if I have a small following?

Yes, Express often values engagement and authenticity over sheer follower count.

### 5. What types of content do influencers create?

Influencers create a range of content including outfit ideas, reviews, and lifestyle posts featuring Express products.

### 6. Is there a contract involved?

Usually, influencers might need to agree to specific terms laid out by Express regarding content creation and brand representation.

### 7. How often should I post?

Regular posting will help maintain audience engagement, though quality should not be sacrificed for quantity.

### 8. Can I collaborate with other brands while being an Express influencer?

Yes, but ensure that collaborations align well with Express's image and values.

### 9. What is the best way to track my performance?

Use analytics tools available on your social media platforms to monitor engagement and adjust strategies accordingly.

### 10. Are there any costs involved in joining the program?

There are typically no costs involved, as Express seeks to empower influencers rather than charge them.
