What is the Amazon Music Influencer Program?
--------------------------------------------

The Amazon Music Influencer Program allows influencers to promote Amazon Music through their channels, providing an opportunity to earn commissions on referrals. With a vast library of songs and playlists, influencers can engage their audiences while driving them towards trying Amazon Music’s subscriptions and services.

How to Get Started

Social Media Presence: A solid following on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or other social networks.

Link Generators: Create custom links to share with your audience.
