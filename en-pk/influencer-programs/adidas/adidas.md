The adidas Affiliate Program is designed to partner with influencers in the United States who want to promote one of the world's leading sports and lifestyle brands. The program allows creators to earn a commission on sales generated through their promotional efforts. With an easy approval process and competitive commission rates, it's a fantastic way for influencers with a minimum of 800 followers to monetize their social media presence.

------------------------

*   **Commission Structure**: Earn a base commission of 0.02% per sale, with top performers earning up to 5.02%.
*   **Cookie Duration**: Enjoy a 30-day cookie period, meaning you can earn commissions on sales made up to 30 days after a referral.
*   **Performance Recognition**: Opportunities for increased commissions based on sales performance.
*   **Brand Association**: Partner with a globally recognized brand that resonates with a diverse audience.
