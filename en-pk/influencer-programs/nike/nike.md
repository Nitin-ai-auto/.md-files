The Nike Affiliate Program enables creators to earn commissions by promoting Nike products across various platforms. ------------------------

*   **Competitive Commission Rates**: Earn a base commission of 0.02% per sale, increasing up to 5.02% for top performers.
*   **Long Cookie Duration**: Benefit from a 30-day cookie window to track referred sales.

*   **Exclusive Promotions and Offers**: Participate in campaigns and exclusive offers to share with your audience.
