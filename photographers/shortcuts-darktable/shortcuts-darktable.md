2. Basic Navigation Shortcuts
-----------------------------

### Essential Navigation Shortcuts

Navigating through Darktable's interface efficiently is crucial. Here are some fundamental shortcuts:

*   Zoom In/Out: Press `Ctrl` + `+` to zoom in and `Ctrl` + `-` to zoom out.
*   Toggle between Modules: Use the `Tab` key to switch between different modules like Library and Darkroom.
*   Navigate Images: Use the arrow keys to navigate between photos in your collection.

### Switching Views

*   Toggle Lighttable/Darkroom: Use `Ctrl + 1` to switch to Lighttable and `Ctrl + 2` for Darkroom.

3. Editing Shortcuts
--------------------

### Color Correction and Adjustments

Speed up your editing tasks with these essential shortcuts for adjustments:

*   Basic Adjustments: `Ctrl` + `A` opens the 'Basic' adjustments panel.
*   Crop Tool: Press `C` to activate the crop tool quickly.
*   Reset Tool: Use `Ctrl` + `R` to reset individual adjustments.

### Filter and Layer Management

*   Add Module: Press `M` to add a new module to the stack.
*   Toggle Module Visibility: Use `V` to toggle the visibility of the active module.

4. Export Shortcuts
-------------------

### Quick Export Options

Once you finish editing, quickly export your images with these shortcuts:

*   Export to Disk: Press `E` to open the export dialogue.
*   Batch Export: Use `Shift + E` to initiate a batch export of selected images.
