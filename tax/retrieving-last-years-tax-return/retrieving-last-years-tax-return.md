How to Get Last Year’s Tax Return: A Complete Guide
---------------------------------------------------

Retrieving a copy of last year’s [tax return](https://joinotto.com/tax-return) can be essential for various reasons, such as applying for loans, preparing the current year’s taxes, or verifying past filings. The IRS provides several options for accessing prior-year tax returns, either as a transcript or a full copy. Understanding the process ensures you can retrieve your tax information quickly and efficiently.

### Why You Might Need Last Year’s Tax Return

Some common reasons for obtaining a prior-year tax return include:

*   Filing current-year taxes and needing to reference previous data.
*   Applying for financial aid, such as completing the FAFSA.
*   Securing loans or mortgages that require proof of income.
*   Resolving disputes with the IRS or state tax agencies.

### Options for Accessing Your Tax Return

The IRS offers two main ways to retrieve information from your past tax returns:

*   **Tax Return Transcript:** A free summary of your return that includes most line items but not detailed attachments. Available for the current tax year and the past three years.
*   **Tax Return Copy:** A full copy of your return, including all attachments, for a fee. Available for the current year and up to six prior years.

### How to Request a Tax Return Transcript

You can obtain a transcript online or by mail:

1.   **Online Request:** Use the IRS "Get Transcript Online" tool. You’ll need to create an account and verify your identity by providing personal information and access to a financial account.
2.   **By Mail:** Use the "Get Transcript by Mail" option on the IRS website or call 1-800-908-9946. Your transcript will arrive within 5–10 business days.

### How to Request a Full Tax Return Copy

To request a complete copy of your tax return:

1.   **Complete Form 4506:** Fill out IRS Form 4506, "Request for Copy of Tax Return," and indicate the specific tax year you need.
2.   **Pay the Fee:** Include a payment of $43 per return (as of 2023). Fee waivers may apply in certain disaster situations.
3.   **Submit the Form:** Mail the completed form and payment to the IRS address provided in the form instructions.

Processing time for full copies is typically 75 calendar days.

### Accessing Tax Returns Through Tax Software

If you filed your taxes using online software, you may be able to retrieve prior-year returns through your account. Log in to the software provider’s platform and navigate to your tax history or archives.

### What to Do If You Can’t Find Your Tax Return

If you did not file a tax return for the year in question, you may need to reconstruct your financial records. Contact employers, financial institutions, or other sources to gather information such as [W-2](https://joinotto.com/tax/w2-filing-deadlines) or [1099](https://joinotto.com/tax-forms/1099).

### Tips for Keeping Tax Records Organized

*   Keep copies of all filed tax returns and supporting documents for at least three years, or longer if required by specific situations.
*   Store digital copies securely using encrypted cloud storage or external hard drives.
*   Label and organize physical records in a dedicated file for easy access.
