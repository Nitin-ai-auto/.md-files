The Jollibee Sponsorship Influencer Program seeks to utilize the growing popularity of Jollibee to create authentic connections with customers across the United States. With a beloved menu that includes Chickenjoy and Jolly Spaghetti, influencers are invited to join in promoting these culinary delights while earning rewards.

 To be eligible, you must be a resident of the U.S. with a minimum of 50 followers on any social media platform. Original content showcasing Jollibee is essential.

**2. How and when do I get paid?**

 Payment details are generally discussed upon acceptance into the program. Payment methods include PayPal, and earnings will vary depending on performance.

**3. How long does the approval process take?**

 The approval time typically ranges from 3 to 5 days after submitting your application.

**4. What content is required?**

 Influencers are encouraged to create authentic, engaging content about Jollibee's menu items, promoting their unique dining experience.

**5. Is support available for program participants?**

 Yes, there is a dedicated support team available for any queries or assistance needed throughout the program.
