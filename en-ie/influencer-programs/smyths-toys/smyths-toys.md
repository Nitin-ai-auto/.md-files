FAQs
----

1.   **How can I qualify for the Smyths Toys Affiliate Program?**

You need a minimum of 10 followers on your social media accounts and must be a resident of the United States to apply.
2.   **When can I expect to receive payments?**

Payments will be processed according to the payment schedule set forth by Smyths Toys, generally through PayPal or bank transfer. Be sure to comply with any tax regulations when you receive your earnings.
3.   **What content is required to promote Smyths toys?**

Influencers are encouraged to create engaging and authentic content that aligns with Smyths' brand. This can include product showcases, reviews, or themed content.
4.   **How do I track my performance as an affiliate?**

You can monitor your clicks, conversions, and commissions through the dedicated affiliate dashboard provided after your application is approved.
5.   **What support is available for affiliates?**

Smyths Toys offers customer support for affiliates and provides marketing materials to help with your promotions.
