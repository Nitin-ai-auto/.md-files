FAQs
----

### What should be included in the agreement?



The agreement should include rental terms, equipment details, rental fees, damage policies, insurance coverage, and return deadlines.

### Can I rent equipment without insurance?



Most agreements require renters to have insurance or a damage waiver to protect the equipment from loss or damage during the rental period.

### How is the rental fee determined?



The rental fee typically depends on the type of equipment, rental duration, and any additional services, such as delivery or setup.

### What happens if the equipment is damaged?



If the equipment is damaged, the renter is usually responsible for repair or replacement costs, as specified in the agreement.

### How long is the rental period?



The rental period is defined in the agreement and can range from a few hours to several weeks, depending on the renter's needs.
