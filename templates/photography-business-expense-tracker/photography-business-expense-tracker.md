### Why do photographers need an expense tracker?



An expense tracker helps photographers monitor costs, maximize deductions, and maintain financial organization, making tax filing easier and improving profitability.

### What expenses should photographers track?



Track costs like camera gear, software, travel, marketing, studio rent, insurance, and client-related expenses to manage finances effectively.

### Can an expense tracker help with tax deductions?



Yes, tracking business expenses ensures you claim all eligible deductions, reducing taxable income and increasing savings.


