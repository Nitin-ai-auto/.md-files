FAQs
----

### What should a photography cancellation policy include?



A cancellation policy should outline deadlines, penalties, and any non-refundable fees. It should specify how cancellations should be communicated and if a deposit is required.

### Can I charge a fee for last-minute cancellations?



Yes, it's common to include a fee for last-minute cancellations to protect your time and income. Specify the time frame for cancellation and the fee structure in your policy.

### Is a deposit refundable if a client cancels?



Typically, deposits are non-refundable, especially if the cancellation occurs within a specified period. Be sure to clarify this in your cancellation policy.

### Can a client reschedule instead of cancelling?



Many photographers allow rescheduling with certain conditions, such as notifying within a specific time frame. Ensure your policy outlines rescheduling options and potential fees.

### What happens if I have to cancel a photoshoot?



If you need to cancel, offer to reschedule or provide a refund if necessary. Include this in your cancellation policy, specifying how cancellations on your end will be handled.
