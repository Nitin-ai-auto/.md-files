To qualify, you need a minimum of 7,000 followers on social media platforms and must comply with Meesho's content guidelines.

Payments are processed through UPI or bank transfers. The payment schedule may vary based on sales cycles and will be detailed in your affiliate account dashboard.

**3. What is the approval process like?**

After submitting your application, it typically takes 3-5 days for approval. You will be notified via email.

**4. Are there any content requirements I should be aware of?**

Yes, your content should comply with Meesho's promotional guidelines, which encourage authenticity and transparency.

Affiliates have access to a dedicated support team and various marketing materials to aid in promoting Meesho's products successfully.
