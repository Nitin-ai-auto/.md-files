Strategies for Success

FAQs
----

**1. What are the eligibility criteria for the Flipkart Affiliate Program?**

To participate, you must have a minimum of 3,900 followers on your social media platforms, showing active and engaging content.

**2. How is payment processed for affiliates?**

Payments are typically made via direct bank transfer or UPI once you reach the minimum payout threshold of ₹500.

**3. How long does the approval process take?**

The typical approval time for applications is between 3 to 5 days, after which eligible influencers will receive notification.

**4. Are there specific content requirements I need to meet?**

Yes, your content should be original, engaging, and promote Flipkart products authentically while complying with FTC guidelines.

**5. What type of support can I expect as an affiliate?**

Affiliates have access to 24/7 dedicated support, along with resources like webinars and marketing materials to help maximize commissions.
