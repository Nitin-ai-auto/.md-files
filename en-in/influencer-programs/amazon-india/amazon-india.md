FAQs
----

**1. What is the minimum follower requirement for the program?**

The minimum follower requirement is 30 on any supported platform, including Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, or ShareChat.

**2. How does the payment process work?**

Payments are made via bank transfer or UPI once you've met the minimum payout threshold, which is determined by your local tax regulations.

**3. What is the approval process like?**

The approval process usually takes 3–5 business days and can be considered hard depending on your content and follower engagement.

**4. Are there specific content requirements?**

Yes, you should ensure that your content adheres to Amazon's guidelines and aligns with their brand values. Engaging content that encourages followers to purchase through your links is essential.

**5. What kind of support does Amazon offer?**

Amazon provides a dedicated support team for influencers, along with an analytics dashboard to help track your performance.
