FAQs
----

**1. What are the eligibility criteria for the Sugar Cosmetics Brand Ambassador Program?**

To be eligible, you need a minimum of 100 followers and an active presence on platforms like Instagram, YouTube, or ShareChat.

**2. How are payments processed?**

Payments are typically made through UPI or bank transfers, depending on your preference.

**3. What is the approval process like?**

After submitting your application through the program URL, you will receive feedback within 3–5 days regarding your approval status.

**4. Are there any specific content requirements?**

Yes, content should be engaging, authentic, and reflective of your personal experience with Sugar products. Influencers are encouraged to avoid stock images and emphasize their unique style.

**5. What kind of support can I expect after joining the program?**

You'll have access to an account manager, an analytics dashboard for tracking performance, and numerous promotional materials to assist your campaigns.
