### What is a 1099 form, and do I need one?



A 1099 form is an IRS document used to report non‐employee compensation and other types of miscellaneous income. If you’re a freelancer, contractor, or self-employed individual who received $600 or more from a client in a single [tax](https://joinotto.com/tax) year, that client is required to issue you a 1099 (typically 1099-NEC or 1099-MISC). Otto AI 1099 form generator helps you generate and manage these forms quickly so you meet your IRS obligations.
