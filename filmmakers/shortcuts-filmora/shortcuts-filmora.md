Common Filmora Shortcuts
------------------------

### Basic Operations

*   New Project: `Ctrl + N`
*   Open Project: `Ctrl + O`
*   Save Project: `Ctrl + S`

These shortcuts are fundamental for managing your projects efficiently. Quickly start new projects and access existing ones with just a few keystrokes.

### Edit Tools

*   Cut: `Ctrl + X`
*   Copy: `Ctrl + C`
*   Paste: `Ctrl + V`
*   Undo: `Ctrl + Z`
*   Redo: `Ctrl + Y`

Knowing these editing tool shortcuts helps maintain your momentum.

### Playback Controls

*   Play/Pause: `Spacebar`
*   Stop Playback: `K`
*   Go to Start: `Home`
*   Go to End: `End`
*   Fast Forward: `L`
*   Rewind: `J`

These playback controls provide seamless navigation through your projects, allowing instant feedback on edits made.

### Media and Timeline Management

*   Zoom In/Out on Timeline: `Ctrl + Plus/Minus` (or `Cmd` on Mac)
*   Align Tracks: `Alt + Right Click`
*   Add New Video Track: Right-click on the timeline

Efficient timeline management is crucial for complex video projects, and these shortcuts will make organizing clips easier.

Customizing Your Shortcuts
--------------------------

Filmora allows users to customize keyboard shortcuts according to their preferences. To do this:

1.   Go to File > Preferences > Shortcuts.
2.   Choose the command you wish to change and assign a new key.
3.   Save your changes.

Custom shortcuts can vastly improve your editing workflow, especially if you’re using specific tools frequently.
