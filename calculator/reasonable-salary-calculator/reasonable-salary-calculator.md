### What is a reasonable salary for an S Corp owner?



A reasonable salary is the amount an S Corporation owner would pay themselves for the work they perform, based on industry standards, duties, experience, and business profits. It must be justifiable to the IRS to avoid underpayment of employment taxes.

### Why does the IRS require S Corp owners to take a salary?



The IRS requires S Corp owners to take a reasonable salary to ensure Social Security and Medicare taxes are paid. If an owner only takes distributions and avoids salary, it can trigger audits and penalties.
