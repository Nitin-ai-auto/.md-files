### What is a break-even calculator used for?



A break-even calculator helps you determine the exact sales volume or revenue you need to cover your total costs, so you know when your business will start making a profit.

### How do I calculate my break-even point?



You calculate your break-even point by dividing your fixed costs by your contribution margin (sales price minus variable cost per unit). The calculator does this instantly for you.

### What inputs do I need for a break-even point calculator?



You’ll need your fixed costs (e.g., rent, salaries), variable cost per unit (e.g., materials), and your sales price per unit to get an accurate break-even point.

### Can the break-even calculator show both units and revenue?



Yes, Otto AI break-even calculator gives you your break-even point in both the number of units you need to sell and the total revenue required to cover your costs.

### Why is knowing my break-even point important?



Knowing your break-even point helps you set prices, forecast profits, plan sales targets, and make better decisions about cost control and investments.

### Is Otto AI break-even analysis calculator free to use?

### Can I use this calculator for service-based businesses?



Absolutely! Service providers can use the calculator by entering fixed and variable costs related to delivering their services, just like product-based businesses.

### Does the calculator account for different pricing scenarios?



Yes, you can quickly adjust your sales price and costs in the calculator to see how changes affect your break-even point, helping you make informed pricing decisions.
