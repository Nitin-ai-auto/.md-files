### What’s Included in a South Carolina Paycheck?

Knowing what goes into your paycheck—and what comes out—is the key to budgeting confidently. Otto AI helps you account for federal and state taxes, plus any deductions related to your benefits, retirement contributions, or local tax laws.

*   **Federal Income Tax**

Calculated based on IRS tax brackets (10% to 37%). Factors in your filing status, number of dependents, and withholdings. Adjusted annually for inflation.
*   **FICA Taxes**

Mandatory for all working Americans: Social Security: 6.2% of wages (up to wage base limit); Medicare: 1.45% of wages; Additional Medicare Tax of 0.9% on wages above $200,000 (single filers).
*   **South Carolina State Income Tax**

South Carolina uses a progressive income tax system: Rates start at 0% and go up to 6.5% based on your taxable income. For example: $0–$3,200: 0%; $3,201–$16,040: scaled rates; Over $16,040: 6.5%. Otto AI automatically applies the latest SC tax brackets to give you an accurate estimate.
*   **Pre-Tax Contributions**

Save more by reducing taxable income with: 401(k) and 403(b) retirement plans; Health Savings Accounts (HSA); Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA); Employer-sponsored insurance premiums. These deductions lower your taxable income and increase your net take-home pay.
