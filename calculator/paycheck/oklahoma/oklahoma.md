### Understanding Your Oklahoma Paycheck

Before your paycheck reaches you, several mandatory deductions reduce your gross earnings. Otto AI factors in all of them to deliver a real-time Oklahoma take-home pay calculator experience.

*   **Federal Income Tax**

With rates ranging from 10% to 37%, federal income tax is calculated based on your income level and filing status.
*   **FICA Taxes (Social Security & Medicare)**

These mandatory contributions include: 6.2% for Social Security (up to a wage limit); 1.45% for Medicare; Additional 0.9% Medicare tax on income over $200,000 (for individuals).
*   **Oklahoma State Income Tax**

Oklahoma has a graduated tax system. Rates range from 0.25% to 4.75%. Your tax rate depends on your income level and filing status.
*   **Pre-Tax Deductions**

Your gross income can be reduced by: 401(k) contributions; Health, dental, or vision insurance premiums; Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) or Health Savings Accounts (HSA). These deductions are factored in by Otto AI Oklahoma salary calculator to provide a more accurate picture of your net pay.
