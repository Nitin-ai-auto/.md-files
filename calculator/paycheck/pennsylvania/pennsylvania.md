### Understanding Your Paycheck in Pennsylvania

Your paycheck isn't just about gross income—it's about what's left after federal, state, and local taxes are withheld. Otto AI smart paycheck estimator includes all relevant deductions so you can see the full picture.

*   **Federal Income Tax**

Federal tax rates range from 10% to 37%, depending on your income and filing status. Standard federal deductions and exemptions apply, with the IRS updating thresholds each year.
*   **FICA Taxes**

Mandatory federal payroll taxes include: Social Security: 6.2% of wages (up to a limit); Medicare: 1.45% of wages; Additional Medicare Tax: 0.9% for incomes over $200,000 (single).
*   **Pennsylvania State Income Tax**

Pennsylvania is one of the few states with a flat income tax rate: 3.07% is withheld regardless of income level. Simple and predictable structure with no standard deduction for state income tax.
*   **Local Earned Income Tax (EIT)**

Many municipalities and school districts in Pennsylvania charge an Earned Income Tax (EIT), which usually ranges from: 1% to 3.8%. Based on where you live and work. Otto AI includes these based on your ZIP code.
*   **Pre-Tax Contributions**

Reduce your taxable income by contributing to: 401(k) or 403(b) retirement plans; Health Savings Accounts (HSA); Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA); Employer-sponsored health insurance premiums. Otto AI PA paycheck calculator reflects these deductions accurately.
