### How Your North Dakota Paycheck Is Calculated

Your gross pay may be what you earn on paper, but your net pay is what truly matters. Otto AI paycheck calculator for North Dakota factors in all the components that impact your final take-home amount:

*   **Federal Income Taxes:** Based on IRS tax brackets and filing status, this deduction varies based on your income level, marital status, and claimed allowances.
*   **FICA Taxes:** 6.2% for Social Security and 1.45% for Medicare. High-income earners may also face an additional 0.9% Medicare tax.
*   **North Dakota State Income Tax:** North Dakota has relatively low state income tax rates, ranging from 1.10% to 2.90% for 2025 depending on your income bracket.
*   **No Local Taxes:** North Dakota does not have local income taxes, making tax calculations a bit more straightforward.
*   **Pre-Tax Contributions:** Voluntary deductions like 401(k), health insurance premiums, HSA, or FSA plans also reduce your taxable income.
